Safety & Policies

Designed for CCNY students — meet safely, stay smart.

How It Works

  1. Sign in with CCNY email (prototype: front-end only).
  2. Post items with clear photos, prices, and meet-up locations.
  3. Coordinate on campus in public, well-lit areas during hours.

Meet-up Guidelines

  • Prefer locations like NAC Lobby, Student Center, or Towers Lobby.
  • Bring a friend for high-value exchanges.
  • Inspect items before paying; cash or mobile P2P at your discretion.

Community Rules

  • No prohibited items (weapons, illegal goods, academic dishonesty materials).
  • Listings must be honest and accurately described.
  • Report suspicious activity to campus security and remove listings if needed.

This is a coursework prototype. No real moderation or payments are connected.
